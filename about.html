About Us

Home About us

We enrich the intellectual growth of our children.

White Cotton School is an independent school for boys and girls that provides a fun, friendly and challenging environment for children in the age range of 18 months to 11 years. We pride ourselves on providing a homely and nurturing environment – where every child feels safe, happy and enjoy their first experience of school. The School occupies a beautiful architectural building which retains its homely atmosphere. We limit our intake, ensuring that class numbers are small enough to allow close personal attention to be given to each and every pupil, an approach which is reflected in our excellent examination results.
